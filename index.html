THE CANSAT PROJECT

The CanSat Project is more than just an educational experience: it is a true miniature space mission. Students from all over the world are challenged to design, build, launch, and operate an actual miniature satellite, the CanSat, replicating the phases of a real space mission: Design, Construction, Launch, and Mission.
