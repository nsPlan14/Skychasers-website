Nizar Souit

Project Manager

Nizar is passionate about the world of space, from engineering to the discoveries of the universe, and he has decided to participate in this competition to connect even more with this passion. He is currently studying computer science and has previously created and developed several electronics projects, starting with the simple yet essential Arduino Uno and reaching the current and powerful ESP32. In this project, he will be responsible for developing the software for the control algorithms and will also dedicate time to electronics by designing the satellite’s Flight Computer. According to him, “if humans reached the Moon with a 2 MHz computer, then with an Arduino Uno, anything is possible.”
